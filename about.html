About the Author

Even though the author of the painting prefers to stay incognito, there is a little information revealed. It a young woman living in Ukraine, who finds her insipation in couples who are in love.

Some people, who know the author say that she is a real talent, and expect to see her painting in the most popular art galleries all over the world
